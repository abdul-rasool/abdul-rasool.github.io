Abdur Rasool, PhD

About Me

Abdur Rasool is a Postdoctoral Associate at the University of Hawaii, U.S.A. He received a PhD in computer applied technology (in 2023) from Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (SIAT-CAS), Shenzhen, China. With research experience at:

  • University of Hawaii, USA (1 year)
  • SIAT-CAS, Shenzhen (3 years)
  • Donghua University (1.5 years)

He has authored over 30 publications in international journals and academic conferences in the data sciences domain, particularly in DNA data storage, bioinformatics, information security, and text mining. He was an excellent international student and an outstanding graduate of the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and a winner of various research contests. His interests are in multidisciplinary computer science and technology, mainly in AI, Bioinformatics, DNA data storage, and LLMs.
